About us

Questoria is a team of enthusiasts who create live role-playing games, where participants become the heroes of the story.

We believe that the best stories are the ones you don't just watch — you live them. That’s why every questoria is built as an immersive experience where decisions matter, characters evolve, and no two games are ever the same.

Our team combines storytelling, game design, and a passion for human connection to create worlds where anyone can step out of everyday life and into an unforgettable adventure.
